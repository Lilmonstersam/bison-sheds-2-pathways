Custom shed projects

Some sheds need more than a kit and a price.

A custom shed often has to protect stock, keep machinery moving, support dairy workflow, meet council requirements and fit the site properly. Bison helps you think through those details before the shed is ordered.

  • Best for farm sheds, hay sheds, machinery sheds, dairy and livestock structures
  • Useful when council approval, site preparation or installation coordination matters
  • Quoted around the job, because site conditions and scope can change what is required
